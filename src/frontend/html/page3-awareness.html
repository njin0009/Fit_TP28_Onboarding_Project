Your UV Risk Profile at UV 9
Melanin provides some natural UV protection, but does not prevent DNA damage. At UV 9, even dark skin tones sustain cellular damage within 20–30 minutes unprotected.
  • Apply SPF 30–50+ daily, even on overcast days
